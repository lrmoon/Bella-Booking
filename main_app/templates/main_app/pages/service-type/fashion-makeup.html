What Are Combo Brows?

Combo brows, often referred to as "combination brows," are a versatile semi-permanent makeup technique that combines the best of both worlds: the soft, shaded effect of ombre brows and the hair-like strokes of microblading. This fusion results in beautifully defined, yet naturally textured eyebrows.


Why Choose Combo Brows?

  • Customized Beauty: Achieve brows tailored to your unique face shape, featuring the ideal combination of shading and microblading for a personalized look.
  • Timeless Elegance: Enjoy long-lasting, low-maintenance brows that enhance your beauty for up to two years.
  • Natural Appearance: Combo brows mimic the appearance of real hair and create a soft, gradient effect that complements your features.
  • Expert Technique: Our esthetician, Bella, is a master of this art, ensuring your comfort and satisfaction throughout the process.
